# Job Description: EMS Program Specialist

### Clinical Quality & Regulatory Compliance Lead

**Organization:** Seven Mountains EMS Council

**Location:** Bellefonte, PA (Serving Centre, Clinton, Columbia, Juniata, Mifflin, Montour, Northumberland, Snyder, and Union Counties)

**Reports To:** Regional Director

**Classification:** Administrative / Regional Support Staff

***

## I. Position Purpose

The **EMS Program Specialist** serves as the primary regional administrator for clinical quality, regulatory oversight, and system-wide compliance for 90 EMS agencies across a 9-county region. This role acts as a critical liaison between the Pennsylvania Department of Health (Bureau of EMS), Regional/Agency Medical Directors, and emergency service providers to ensure adherence to **Act 37** and **Title 28** regulations. The Specialist is responsible for maintaining the clinical integrity of the regional EMS system through rigorous data analysis, investigative oversight, and licensure management.

## II. Key Areas of Responsibility

### 1. Clinical Quality & Medical Advisory (MAC/CQI)

-   **Medical Advisory Committee (MAC) Staff Lead:** Coordinate the regional MAC, facilitating collaboration between the Regional Medical Director and over 90 Agency Medical Directors to develop and implement clinical protocols.
    
-   **CQI Leadership:** Direct the regional Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI) committee; develop benchmarks for clinical excellence and monitor system-wide patient care trends.
    
-   **Data Analysis:** Review electronic Patient Care Reports (ePCR) to identify clinical gaps, medication trends, and protocol compliance, providing actionable reports to stakeholders.
    

### 2. Regulatory Compliance & Investigations

-   **Investigatory Lead:** Manage the intake and processing of formal complaints and clinical inquiries. Conduct comprehensive investigations into alleged violations of the EMS Systems Act (Act 37) and Title 28.
    
-   **Evidence Coordination:** Gather evidence, conduct interviews, and prepare detailed findings for the Bureau of EMS, ensuring all proceedings follow PA state law and "due process" mandates.
    
-   **Risk Mitigation:** Interpret complex state regulations to advise agencies on compliance strategies, mitigating the risk of state-level sanctions or litigation.
    

### 3. Agency Licensure & Inspection

-   **Licensure Management:** Oversee the regional licensure cycle for 90 EMS agencies (QRS, BLS, and ALS levels).
    
-   **Technical Inspections:** Conduct on-site inspections of EMS vehicles, equipment caches, and controlled substance storage to verify compliance with the PA Department of Health "Required Equipment List."
    
-   **Credentialing Oversight:** Verify agency staffing levels and ensure that all personnel maintain valid, state-mandated certifications.
    

### 4. PA CARES Coordination

-   **Registry Management:** Serve as the Regional Coordinator for the **Cardiac Arrest Registry to Enhance Survival (CARES)**.
    
-   **Outcome Integration:** Bridge the data gap between EMS agencies and receiving hospitals to track survival-to-discharge metrics, providing the region with critical life-saving performance data.
    

### 5. Regional Preparedness

-   **MCI Planning:** Assist in the development and maintenance of Regional Mass Casualty Incident (MCI) plans and Disaster Operating Guidelines.
    
-   **Resource Coordination:** Manage regional resource preparedness tasks, including the oversight of specialized equipment and coordination of regional EMS Strike Teams.
    

***

## III. Core Competencies & Qualifications

-   **Regulatory Expertise:** Master-level knowledge of **PA Title 28, Part VII** and **Act 37**, with the ability to translate legal mandates into operational workflows.
    
-   **Analytical Acumen:** Proficient in data-driven decision-making, utilizing ePCR and CARES registries to drive clinical improvement.
    
-   **Stakeholder Management:** Exceptional ability to manage relationships with physicians, agency executives, and state regulators.
    
-   **Professional Integrity:** Ability to handle sensitive investigations with strict confidentiality and impartiality.
    

***

## IV. Scope of Impact

This position is responsible for the regulatory health of the EMS system in a diverse nine-county region. Success in this role ensures that over 90 independent services operate within the law and provide high-quality medical care to the citizens of Central Pennsylvania.
